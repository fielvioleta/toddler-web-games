Save your 4 downloaded animal sound files in THIS folder.

Rename them EXACTLY like this (all lowercase, .mp3):

  cat.mp3     <- the cat / meow sound
  dog.mp3     <- the barking dog sound
  cow.mp3     <- the cow moo sound
  bird.mp3    <- the bird singing sound

That's it. After the files are here, refresh the Animal Sounds game and they will play.

If your files are a different format (like .wav or .ogg), tell me and I'll adjust the code.
